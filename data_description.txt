Author : Luna McBride

URL : https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lunamcbride24/pokemon-type-matchup-data?resource=download

Number of Nodes:577
Number of Edges: NA

Other Qualities: Directed Value Graph. Self Edges not allowed. 

Additional Information: The data shows the different multipliers, with a multiplier of 2 meaning the pokemon is more powerful and 0.5 as a weaker multiplier.
In our graph, we only calculated for gen01 pokemons with a total of 19 nodes for each graph. 51 edges for strength graph and 61 edges for weakness graph.
